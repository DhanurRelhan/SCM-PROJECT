and SEO best practices, making it a powerful tool for professionals to get noticed and hired. This innovative solution is        |
the brainchild of a talented team of creators - Dhanur, Anshika, Anshul, and Dhruv. Their combined expertise and vision have     |
culminated in a platform that effectively addresses the challenges faced by professionals in showcasing their work online.       |
“Rezume” is more than just a portfolio website; it’s a step towards empowering professionals in the digital age.                 |
